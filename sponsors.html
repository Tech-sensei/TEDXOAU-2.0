Why Your Support Matters

TEDxOAU is a student-run, non-profit organization. Therefore, your partnership with TEDxOAU ensures the success of our event, allowing us to provide attendees with an exceptional and unique experience. The best to be ever experienced on OAU campus and its environs. We want to create a platform where ideas could be exchanged freely and openly; ideas that could form the foundation for innovation; ideas which could help shape tomorrow's world today. With the Theme of "Stories of Change," we aim to let our audience witness the changemakers recounting their ideas for persistence, perseverance, and ultimately excellence.

Taking this in mind, we want the event which will be hosted on Obafemi Awolowo University's campus to show first hand how much potential the institution has, has produced and how much we can do with it. Most importantly, we want everyone who will attend TEDxOAU to leave with a positive experience and a renewed sense of purpose and optimism about themselves and the world they live in. Whatever it may be, we want everyone who attends TEDxOAU to leave feeling inspired, empowered, and ready to take laudable action!
